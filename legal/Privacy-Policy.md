# Privacy Policy

- **Effective Date:** 03/20/2026
- **Last Updated:** 03/20/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how OverThink ("OverThink", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the OverThink app, subscription features, and related legal pages.

## **Scope**

  - This Policy applies to OverThink mobile app usage and OverThink+ subscription-related processing.
  - Third-party platforms and services (including Apple and your email provider) are governed by their own privacy policies.

## **Information We Collect**

**Account and Authentication Information**
  - OverThink uses Sign in with Apple.
  - Apple may provide a stable account identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address.
  - We do not collect or store account passwords.

**Check-In and App Content**
  - You may enter thoughts, reflections, check-in responses, distress ratings, and similar app content.
  - When iCloud sync is enabled, synced content is stored in Apple CloudKit and associated with your iCloud account.
  - Synced content is encrypted in transit and at rest through Apple's infrastructure.
  - OverThink also applies app-level encryption to cloud-synced payloads before upload.
  - On-device session storage is encrypted and protected by iOS data protection controls.

**Subscription and Purchase Information**
  - OverThink+ purchases are processed by Apple through in-app purchase.
  - We may receive limited subscription metadata from Apple, including product identifiers, trial status, entitlement status, renewal status, transaction references, and expiration dates.
  - We do **not** receive full payment card numbers, bank account numbers, or complete payment credentials.

**Usage, Diagnostics, and Analytics**
  - We collect limited analytics and diagnostic information to operate and improve the app.
  - This may include feature usage, reliability signals, app events, and crash/performance information.
  - Analytics may be processed through Cloudflare and similar service infrastructure.
  - We do not sell personal information and do not run cross-app advertising tracking.

**Notifications**
  - If you opt in, we may send local or push reminders.
  - Notification permissions are optional and can be changed in device settings.

**Support Communications**
  - The app may open your email app so you can contact us at overthinkapp@gmail.com.
  - If you send us email, we receive the message content and standard email metadata (for example sender address and headers).

## **How We Use Information**

We may use information to:
  - Provide, maintain, secure, and improve OverThink.
  - Deliver core app functions, including check-ins, reminders, sync, and account-linked experiences.
  - Determine OverThink+ subscription access and entitlement state.
  - Diagnose errors, prevent abuse, and maintain app integrity.
  - Respond to support requests, bug reports, and user feedback.
  - Meet legal, regulatory, and compliance obligations.

## **How Subscription Data Is Handled**

  - Subscription billing, cancellation, renewals, and refunds are handled by Apple under Apple's terms.
  - We rely on Apple-provided subscription state data to enable or disable premium features.
  - Billing disputes and refund requests should be submitted through Apple.

## **How We Share Information**

We may share limited information with service providers that support app operations, including:
  - Apple (authentication, CloudKit sync/storage, in-app purchase handling).
  - Cloud and analytics infrastructure providers.
  - Email infrastructure in connection with user-initiated support communications.

We may also disclose information when necessary to:
  - Comply with applicable law, legal process, or enforceable government request.
  - Protect rights, safety, and security of users, us, or others.
  - Support a merger, financing, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or transfer of assets, where permitted by law.

## **Data Retention**

  - We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes in this Policy.
  - Cloud-synced check-in content remains in your CloudKit/iCloud context unless deleted by you or removed under platform behavior.
  - Subscription metadata may be retained for entitlement integrity, fraud prevention, tax/accounting, and legal compliance.
  - Support records may be retained to respond to ongoing issues and maintain service history.

## **Data Security**

  - We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards.
  - Safeguards include encrypted local session storage and encrypted payload handling for cloud-synced content.
  - No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## **International Processing**

  - Depending on where you use OverThink and where service providers operate, information may be processed in countries other than your own.
  - Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border processing.

## **Your Choices and Rights**

  - You can control notification permissions through your device settings.
  - You can manage OverThink+ subscriptions through your Apple account subscription settings.
  - Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, or objection.
  - To submit a privacy request, contact overthinkapp@gmail.com.
  - We may request verification before fulfilling certain requests.

## **Children's Privacy**

  - OverThink is not directed to, and is not intended for use by, children under 9.
  - We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 9.
  - If we learn that we collected personal information from a child under 9, we will delete it as required by law.
  - If you believe a child under 9 has provided information through OverThink, contact overthinkapp@gmail.com.
  - App Store age labels may vary by region or OS version; regardless of platform labeling, OverThink requires users to be at least 9 years old.

## **Not Medical or Crisis Service**

  - OverThink is a self-help tool and is not a medical, mental health, therapy, diagnosis, treatment, or crisis-response service.
  - Do not rely on OverThink as a substitute for professional medical or mental health care.
  - If you are in immediate danger or may harm yourself or others, contact local emergency services immediately.

## **Changes to This Policy**

  - We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
  - Updated versions will be posted on this page with an updated effective/last-updated date.

## **Contact**

  - For privacy questions or requests, contact: overthinkapp@gmail.com
