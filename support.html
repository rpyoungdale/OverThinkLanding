OverThink Support

  • Support Email: overthinkapp@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We typically respond within 1-2 business days.

Need Help?

  • If the app is not working as expected, email us with your app version, iOS version, and a brief description of the issue.
  • Including a screenshot or screen recording helps us troubleshoot faster.
  • For account or sync issues, tell us whether you are using Sign in with Apple and iCloud sync.

Subscription and Billing

  • OverThink+ subscriptions are managed by Apple.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription: Apple Subscriptions
  • Request a refund: reportaproblem.apple.com

Safety Notice

  • OverThink is a self-help tool and does not provide medical or crisis services.
  • If you are in immediate danger or may harm yourself or others, contact local emergency services immediately.
  • In the U.S., call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
